Activation

When you first install DMS, the community version will be the default license. In order to activate your Personal, Professional or Developer license, you will need to perform two tasks, install the DMS Pro Tools plugin, as well as enter your license key and email address.

Pro Tools Plugin

The Pro Tools plugin enables the premium membership features and utilities needed for your Personal, Professional or Developer license.

  • Login to your PageLines Account
  • Next, go to: My Account → My Downloads
  • Then click on the Download Free PRO Tools button to download the plugin.
  • Once downloaded, install the plugin via the Wordpress Administrator Dashboard → Plugins → Add New.
  • Click Upload and locate the plugin's .zip file from your Computer.
  • After the installation process has completed, a Your Account panel will be available under DMS Toolbar → Account.
  • From here, you can enter your License Email address and License key.

Locating your License Key

To find your license key, follow the simple insructions below:

  • Login to your PageLines Account
  • Next, go to: My Account → My Orders, then click the View button.
  • Here you will see your Licence Email, License Key and activations remaining.

Entering your License Key

  • Simply copy your license key, from your orders page.
  • Next, login to your website's Wordpress Administrator Dashboard and visit the front-end of your website.
  • From the DMS Toolbar, navigate to Account → Your Account
  • In the PageLines Account field, enter the email address used to purchase your license and in the Pro Activation Key (optional) enter the license you copied from your orders pages.
  • Now click the Update button, after a few moments, your site will refresh and your site will be active, along with your Personal, Professional or Developer license.

Activation Issues

If you encounter any issues activating your license, please contact our helpdesk.
